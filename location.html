Location

Our clinic is conveniently situated within the vibrant Kanata Tech Park, a hub of technological innovation and business activity. When you arrive at the clinic, you'll find ample parking available just steps away from the entrance, ensuring easy access for everyone.

Once inside, you'll be greeted by our friendly and attentive staff, ready to assist you with any inquiries or concerns you may have. The clinic itself is designed with patient comfort in mind, featuring a calming and tranquil atmosphere to help you feel at ease.

If you're using public transportation, the clinic is conveniently served by several bus routes that pass through the Kanata area as well as the Innovation Park-n-ride located right next to it. The nearby bus stops ensure easy accessibility for those relying on public transit.
